## Archive a Campaign

**Archiving** removes a campaign from your active list without deleting it. You can view archived campaigns anytime and restore them if needed.

1. Go to your **Campaigns** dashboard
2. Find the campaign you want to archive
3. Click the three-dot menu (⋯) on the campaign card
4. Select **Archive**
5. Confirm when prompted

The campaign moves to your archive. It won't appear in your active campaigns list, and you won't receive notifications for it.

### Restore an Archived Campaign

1. Click **Archived** at the top of your Campaigns dashboard
2. Find the campaign you want to restore
3. Click the three-dot menu (⋯)
4. Select **Restore**

The campaign returns to your active list.

## Duplicate a Campaign

**Duplicating** creates an exact copy of a campaign—useful for running similar promotions or testing variations.

1. Go to your **Campaigns** dashboard
2. Click the three-dot menu (⋯) on the campaign you want to copy
3. Select **Duplicate**
4. Edit the campaign name if needed (it defaults to "[Original Name] - Copy")
5. Choose whether to include existing **Performance Data** (optional)
6. Click **Create Duplicate**

The new campaign appears in your active list. All settings, creative assets, and targeting options copy over. You can edit any details before launching.

### Important Notes

- Duplicated campaigns are **paused by default**—activate them when you're ready
- If you skip performance data, the duplicate starts with zero metrics
- Connected ad accounts carry over to the duplicate

## Delete a Campaign

**Deleting** permanently removes a campaign and its data. This action can't be undone.

1. Go to your **Campaigns** dashboard
2. Click the three-dot menu (⋯) on the campaign
3. Select **Delete**
4. Type the campaign name to confirm
5. Click **Delete Campaign**

Once deleted, the campaign is gone—including all performance data, messages, and scheduled posts.

### When to Archive Instead

Consider archiving before deleting. Reasons:

- Keep historical data for reference
- Restore campaigns if plans change
- Maintain a complete record of all work
- Reduce clutter without losing information

Only delete campaigns you're absolutely certain you won't need again.

## Managing Multiple Campaigns

You can perform bulk actions on several campaigns at once:

1. Check the boxes next to the campaigns you want to manage
2. Click the **Bulk Actions** button that appears
3. Choose:
   - **Archive** — move all selected campaigns to archive
   - **Delete** — permanently remove all selected campaigns
4. Confirm the action

Bulk actions save time when cleaning up old campaigns or organizing your workspace.

## Tips for Organization

- **Use clear naming conventions** — include dates, client names, or campaign type so they're easy to find
- **Archive regularly** — keep your active list focused on current work
- **Review archived campaigns** — restore successful templates or reference past performance
- **Set a deletion policy** — decide how long to keep data before permanently removing campaigns
