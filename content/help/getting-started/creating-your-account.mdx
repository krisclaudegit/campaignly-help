## Sign up for Campaignly

Creating a Campaignly account takes just a few minutes. You'll be managing campaigns right away.

## What you need before you start

- A valid email address
- A password (at least 8 characters)
- Basic information about your business or freelance practice

## Create your account

1. Go to [campaignly.com/signup](https://campaignly.com/signup)
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a password (must be at least 8 characters)
4. Click **Sign Up**
5. Check your email for a verification link and click it

Your account is now active. You'll land on the **Welcome** screen.

## Complete your profile

Finish setting up your profile so clients and team members can recognize your account.

1. Click your profile icon in the top right corner
2. Select **Account Settings**
3. Fill in these fields:
   - **Name** — Your full name
   - **Business name** — Your agency or freelance business name
   - **Industry** — Select the category that best fits (marketing, advertising, social media, etc.)
   - **Profile photo** — Upload a headshot or business logo (optional but recommended)
4. Click **Save**

## Set up your workspace

Your **workspace** is where you'll organize clients, campaigns, and team members.

1. From the Welcome screen, click **Create Workspace**
2. Give your workspace a name (e.g., "Smith Marketing Agency")
3. Choose your workspace type:
   - **Agency** — If you manage campaigns for multiple clients
   - **Freelancer** — If you work solo or manage your own projects
4. Click **Create**

You can create multiple workspaces later if needed — each one stays separate, so you can keep client work organized.

## Invite your team

Add team members now or do this later. They'll need email addresses to join.

1. From your workspace, click **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Select **Team Members**
3. Click **Invite Team Member**
4. Enter their email address
5. Choose their **role**:
   - **Admin** — Full access to all settings and campaigns
   - **Editor** — Can create and edit campaigns, can't change workspace settings
   - **Viewer** — Can view campaigns and reports, can't make changes
6. Click **Send Invite**

They'll receive an email and can join your workspace by clicking the link.

## Important notes

- **One email, one account** — You can't use the same email address for multiple Campaignly accounts. Create a workspace if you need to separate different businesses or clients.
- **Free trial** — All new accounts include a 14-day free trial with full access. No credit card required.
- **Upgrade anytime** — When your trial ends, choose a plan or let your account pause. Your campaigns and data stay safe.
