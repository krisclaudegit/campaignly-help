# What Is Campaignly?

Campaignly is a local marketing platform built for **marketing agencies, freelance marketers, and small business owners** who run Google Ads, Meta Ads, SEO, and local marketing campaigns for their clients.

Think of it as command central for your client campaigns — one place to set up campaigns, run audits, track performance, set benchmarks, build proposals, and manage your team.

## Who Is Campaignly For?

Campaignly is designed for two types of users:

**Marketers** are the agencies or freelancers who manage campaigns for their clients. They get access to the full platform: audit tools, benchmark tracking, proposal builder, and integrations.

**Business users** are the clients — small business owners who want visibility into how their campaigns are performing. They get a simplified view focused on their results.

## What Can You Do With Campaignly?

### Run Campaign Audits
Connect your client's Google Ads, GA4, Meta Ads, Google Search Console, and local platforms. Campaignly audits each one and generates a detailed report showing what's working, what's broken, and what to fix first.

### Set Benchmarks and Track KPIs
Define performance targets for each campaign (click-through rate, cost per click, conversion rate, and more). Campaignly grades performance from A to F so you always know where you stand against your targets.

### Build and Export Proposals
Create white-label proposals directly from your campaign data. Export as PDF or save to Google Drive — no extra design work required.

### Manage Your Team
Invite team members, assign roles (admin, member), and work across multiple organizations with a single login.

### Connect All Your Platforms
Campaignly integrates directly with Google (Ads, Analytics 4, Search Console), Meta (Facebook, Instagram), and LinkedIn — with more platforms on the way.

## How Is It Different From Other Tools?

Most marketing tools focus on one platform (e.g., Google Ads reports or GA4 dashboards). Campaignly is designed around the **campaign lifecycle** — from initial setup and audit through benchmarks, reporting, and proposals. It's a workflow tool, not just a dashboard.
