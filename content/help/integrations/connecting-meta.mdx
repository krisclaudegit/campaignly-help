## What this integration does

Connecting your **Meta Business account** to Campaignly lets you create and manage Facebook and Instagram ads directly from the platform. You can launch campaigns, monitor performance, and adjust spend without leaving Campaignly.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have:

- A **Meta Business account** (previously Facebook Business Manager)
- Admin or advertiser access to that account
- At least one **ad account** created within your Meta Business account
- A Campaignly account with appropriate permissions

## Connect your Meta Business account

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** in Campaignly.
2. Find **Meta** (or **Facebook & Instagram**) and click **Connect**.
3. You'll be redirected to Meta's login page. Sign in with your Meta Business account credentials.
4. Review the permissions Campaignly is requesting. Click **Allow** to proceed.
5. Select which ad account(s) you want to connect. You can add multiple accounts if needed.
6. Click **Confirm** to complete the connection.

Campaignly will now sync your ad accounts and you're ready to create campaigns.

## Create an ad campaign

Once connected, you can build Facebook or Instagram ads:

1. In your campaign builder, select **Meta** as your platform.
2. Choose your **ad account** from the dropdown.
3. Select your audience, creative assets, and campaign objective.
4. Set your budget and schedule.
5. Review and publish.

Your ad goes live on Facebook and/or Instagram based on your placements.

## Disconnect your account

To remove the connection:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**.
2. Find Meta and click the menu icon (⋮).
3. Select **Disconnect**.
4. Confirm the action.

This disconnects Campaignly's access to your ad accounts. Any active campaigns managed through Campaignly will stop being manageable from the platform, but won't be paused or deleted.

## Troubleshooting

**"Permission denied" error during connection**

You likely don't have admin access to the Meta Business account. Ask your account owner or someone with admin rights to complete the connection step.

**Ad account doesn't appear in the dropdown**

The account may not be linked to your Meta Business account yet. Go to Meta Business Manager, add the ad account, then return to Campaignly and try connecting again.

**Connected account is missing recent data**

Meta's data can take 24–48 hours to sync fully. Wait a day and refresh your dashboard.

**I want to connect a different ad account**

Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** → **Meta**, then click **Manage Accounts**. You can add or remove ad accounts without disconnecting entirely.

## Important notes

- Campaignly syncs performance data every few hours. Real-time reporting isn't available.
- Ads created outside Campaignly (directly in Meta) won't appear in Campaignly's campaign list.
- You can't edit ads after they've launched through Campaignly. You'll need to pause and create a new one.
- Ensure your payment method is set up in Meta before launching campaigns.
