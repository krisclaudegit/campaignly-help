## Overview

Connecting your Google account to Campaignly lets you pull data from **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)**, **Google Ads**, and **Google Search Console** directly into your campaigns and reports. Once connected, you can view performance metrics without leaving Campaignly.

## What you need

- A Google account with access to the properties you want to track
- Admin or Editor permissions on the Google Analytics 4 property, Google Ads account, or Search Console domain
- Your Campaignly account should have Admin permissions

## How to connect

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Find **Google** and click **Connect**
3. You'll be redirected to Google's login page
4. Sign in with the Google account that has access to your properties
5. Review the permissions Campaignly is requesting and click **Allow**
6. You'll return to Campaignly. Your account is now connected.

## Selecting which properties to sync

After connecting, you'll choose which Google properties to add to Campaignly:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** → **Google**
2. Under each service (GA4, Google Ads, Search Console), click **Add Property**
3. Select the property you want to sync
4. Click **Confirm**

You can add multiple properties. Campaignly will start pulling data within a few minutes.

## Using your connected data

Once properties are synced:

- **GA4 data** appears in campaign reports and performance dashboards
- **Google Ads data** shows spend, clicks, and conversions in your campaign overview
- **Search Console data** displays impressions and click-through rates for organic search

Use these metrics to compare performance across channels in one place.

## Disconnecting or removing properties

To stop syncing a specific property:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** → **Google**
2. Find the property under its service
3. Click the **Remove** icon
4. Confirm the action

To disconnect your entire Google account:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Click **Disconnect** next to Google
3. Confirm

This stops all Google data from syncing into Campaignly.

## Common issues

**"Permission denied" when connecting:**
Make sure you're signing in with a Google account that has admin access to the properties you want to sync. Personal Gmail accounts often don't have these permissions.

**Data isn't showing up:**
- Check that the property was properly added in the **Integrations** settings
- Allow up to 24 hours for historical data to backfill
- Confirm the property is receiving data in Google's native platforms (GA4, Google Ads, or Search Console)

**I see data from properties I didn't select:**
Campaignly only syncs properties you explicitly add. If you see unexpected data, check your **Integrations** settings to see what's connected.

**Need to reconnect with a different Google account:**
Disconnect your current account, then follow the connection steps above with a different Google account.
